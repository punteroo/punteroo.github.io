About Me

My name is Lucas Maza, I'm a 19 years old freelance programmer and engineering student from Argentina. I love working on SourceMod plugins, specifically Team Fortress 2 ones, and developing lots of back-end services even though I aspire to become a Full-Stack dev one day.

I like playing videogames on spare time, learn new programming technologies and workflow techniques. I try to learn as fast as possible whenever a new framework or API shows up at my workspace, and I kinda get the hang of it in a little while! (at least the basics :P)

My main programming languages are JavaScript, PHP, SourcePawn and a bit of Python. I'm planning on learning C#, TypeScript and probably Go.

I'm very familiar with the runtime environment NodeJS, and frameworks such as ExpressJS and (learning) Electron. One unfinished project I was working on is called CustomTF, a TF2 Virtual Inventory that is separate from the In-Game one. Players would be able to equip weapons, cosmetics, craft, uncrate and everything else that could be done on the real thing. It is scrapped for now.

I have intermediate experience working with MySQL databases and basic knowledge of NoSQL ones. I've utilized MariaDB and MongoDB so far.

I have plans on collaborating in Open Source projects once I feel like my experience is high enough for it. I'm still learning!

And that's about it! Thank you for reading!
